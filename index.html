Emergency Preparedness Guides

Cyclone Preparation

  • Secure loose outdoor items.
  • Trim tree branches around your home.
  • Prepare an evacuation plan.
  • Keep your emergency kit stocked and ready.

Flood Safety Tips

  • Never walk or drive through floodwaters.
  • Move valuables to higher ground.
  • Disconnect electrical appliances.
  • Listen to emergency broadcasts for updates.

Bushfire Survival Guide

  • Clear leaves and debris from gutters.
  • Have a bushfire survival plan ready.
  • Stay informed with official alerts.
  • Wear protective clothing when outside.
