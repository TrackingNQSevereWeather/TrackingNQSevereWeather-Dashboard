🧰 Emergency Preparedness Guides

🌀 Cyclone Preparation

  • Trim trees and secure loose items around your home.
  • Have an evacuation plan and know your zone.
  • Store food, water, and emergency supplies for 3 days.

🌊 Flood Safety Tips

  • Never drive or walk through floodwaters.
  • Move valuables and electronics to higher ground.
  • Prepare sandbags if flooding is expected.

🔥 Bushfire Survival Guide

  • Clear gutters and remove dry vegetation around your home.
  • Have a bushfire survival plan ready for your family.
  • Listen to emergency broadcasts and leave early if needed.
